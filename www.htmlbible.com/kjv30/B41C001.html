Chapter 1

1

The beginning of the gospel of Jesus Christ, the Son of God;

2

As it is written in the prophets, Behold, I send my messenger before thy face, which shall prepare thy way before thee.
Behold Mal 3:1, Matt 11:10, Luke 7:27

3

The voice of one crying in the wilderness, Prepare ye the way of the Lord, make his paths straight.
The voice Isa 40:3, Matt 3:3, Luke 1:17, Luke 1:76, Luke 3:4, John 1:23

4

John did baptize in the wilderness, and preach the baptism of repentance for the remission of sins.
John Mal 4:5, Matt 3:1, Luke 3:2, John 1:28

5

And there went out unto him all the land of Judaea, and they of Jerusalem, and were all baptized of him in the river of Jordan, confessing their sins.
went Matt 3:5, Luke 3:3

6

And John was clothed with camel's hair, and with a girdle of a skin about his loins; and he did eat locusts and wild honey;
clothed 2nd Kings 1:8 Zech 13:4 Matt 3:4 Matt 11:8
locusts Lev 11:22, Matt 3:4

7

And preached, saying, There cometh one mightier than I after me, the latchet of whose shoes I am not worthy to stoop down and unloose.

8

I indeed have baptized you with water: but he shall baptize you with the Holy Ghost.
water Matt 3:11, Luke 3:16, John 1:26

9

And it came to pass in those days, that Jesus came from Nazareth of Galilee, and was baptized of John in Jordan.
baptized Matt 3:13 Luke 3:21 John 1:29

10

And straightway coming up out of the water, he saw the heavens opened, and the Spirit like a dove descending upon him:
descending Isa 11:2, Matt 3:16, Luke 3:22, John 1:32,33

11

And there came a voice from heaven, saying, Thou art my beloved Son, in whom I am well pleased.
voice John 12:28
Son Psa 2:7, Matt 3:17

12

And immediately the spirit driveth him into the wilderness.
driveth Matt 4:1, Luke 4:1

13

And he was there in the wilderness forty days, tempted of Satan; and was with the wild beasts; and the angels ministered unto him.
tempted Matt 4:1, Luke 4:2, Heb 4:15

14

Now after that John was put in prison, Jesus came into Galilee, preaching the gospel of the kingdom of God,
Galilee Matt 4:12, Luke 4:14, John 4:43

15

And saying, The time is fulfilled, and the kingdom of God is at hand: repent ye, and believe the gospel.

16

Now as he walked by the sea of Galilee, he saw Simon and Andrew his brother casting a net into the sea: for they were fishers.
Simon Matt 4:18 Luke 5:2 John 1:40

17

And Jesus said unto them, Come ye after me, and I will make you to become fishers of men.
Come Matt 4:19, Luke 5:10

18

And straightway they forsook their nets, and followed him.
forsook Matt 4:20, 22, 19:27, Mark 1:20, 10:28, Luke 5:11, 18:28

19

And when he had gone a little farther thence, he saw James the son of Zebedee, and John his brother, who also were in the ship mending their nets.
Zebedee Matt 4:21, Matt 10:2, Matt 20:20, Matt 26:37, Mark 10:35

20

And straightway he called them: and they left their father Zebedee in the ship with the hired servants, and went after him.
left Matt 4:20, 22, 19:27, Mark 1:18, 10:28, Luke 5:11, 18:28

21

And they went into Capernaum; and straightway on the sabbath day he entered into the synagogue, and taught.
Capernaum Matt 4:13, Matt 9:1, Matt 11:23, Mark 2:1, Luke 4:23, Luke 4:31, John 2:12

22

And they were astonished at his doctrine: for he taught them as one that had authority, and not as the scribes.
astonished Matt 7:28, Matt 13:54, Matt 22:33, Mark 6:2, Luke 4:32

23

And there was in their synagogue a man with an unclean spirit; and he cried out,

24

Saying, Let us alone; what have we to do with thee, thou Jesus of Nazareth? art thou come to destroy us? I know thee who thou art, the Holy One of God.
Nazareth Num 6:2, Jud 13:5, 1st Sam 1:11, Isa 11:1, Matt 2:23, Luke 1:26

25

And Jesus rebuked him, saying, Hold thy peace, and come out of him.

26

And when the unclean spirit had torn him, and cried with a loud voice, he came out of him.

27

And they were all amazed, insomuch that they questioned among themselves, saying, What thing is this? what new doctrine is this? for with authority commandeth he even the unclean spirits, and they do obey him.

28

And immediately his fame spread abroad throughout all the region round about Galilee.

29

And forthwith, when they were come out of the synagogue, they entered into the house of Simon and Andrew, with James and John.
house Matt 8:14, Luke 4:38

30

But Simon's wife's mother lay sick of a fever, and anon they tell him of her.

31

And he came and took her by the hand, and lifted her up; and immediately the fever left her, and she ministered unto them.

32

And at even, when the sun did set, they brought unto him all that were diseased, and them that were possessed with devils.
even Matt 8:16, Luke 4:40

33

And all the city was gathered together at the door.

34

And he healed many that were sick of divers diseases, and cast out many devils; and suffered not the devils to speak, because they knew him.
healed Matt 4:24, Matt 9:35
divers

35

And in the morning, rising up a great while before day, he went out, and departed into a solitary place, and there prayed.
prayed Gen 24:63, Dan 6:10, Luke 5:16, Luke 6:12, Acts 10:9

36

And Simon and they that were with him followed after him.

37

And when they had found him, they said unto him, All men seek for thee.

38

And he said unto them, Let us go into the next towns, that I may preach there also: for therefore came I forth.

39

And he preached in their synagogues throughout all Galilee, and cast out devils.

40

And there came a leper to him, beseeching him, and kneeling down to him, and saying unto him, If thou wilt, thou canst make me clean.
wilt Matt 8:2, Luke 5:12

41

And Jesus, moved with compassion, put forth his hand, and touched him, and saith unto him, I will; be thou clean.
compassion Psa 86:15, Psa 111:4, Psa 145:8, Matt 9:36, Matt 14:14, Heb 2:17, Heb 4:15, Heb 5:2

42

And as soon as he had spoken, immediately the leprosy departed from him, and he was cleansed.

43

And he straitly charged him, and forthwith sent him away;

44

And saith unto him, See thou say nothing to any man: but go thy way, shew thyself to the priest, and offer for thy cleansing those things which Moses commanded, for a testimony unto them.

45

But he went out, and began to publish it much, and to blaze abroad the matter, insomuch that Jesus could no more openly enter into the city, but was without in desert places: and they came to him from every quarter.
