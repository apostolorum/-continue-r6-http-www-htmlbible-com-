Chapter 11

1

In the LORD put I my trust: how say ye to my soul, Flee as a bird to your mountain?

2

For, lo, the wicked bend their bow, they make ready their arrow upon the string, that they may privily shoot at the upright in heart.

3

If the foundations be destroyed, what can the righteous do?

4

The LORD is in his holy temple, the LORD's throne is in heaven: his eyes behold, his eyelids try, the children of men.

5

The LORD trieth the righteous: but the wicked and him that loveth violence his soul hateth.
trieth Gen 18:23, Num 16:22, 2nd Sam 24:17

6

Upon the wicked he shall rain snares, fire and abrimstone, and an horrible tempest: this shall be the portion of their cup.
rain Gen 19:24, Deut 29:23, Isa 13:19, Jer 20:16, Jer 50:40, Eze 16:50, Amos 4:11, Zeph 2:9, Luke 17:29, 2nd Pet 2:6, Jude 7

7

For the righteous LORD loveth righteousness; his countenance doth behold the upright.
