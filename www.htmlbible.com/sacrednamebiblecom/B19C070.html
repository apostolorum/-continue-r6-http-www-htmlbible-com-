Chapter 70

1

MAKE HASTE, O ELOHIYM, TO DELIVER ME; MAKE HASTE TO HELP ME, O YHVH.

2

Let them be ashamed and confounded that seek after my soul: let them be turned backward, and put to confusion, that desire my hurt.

3

Let them be turned back for a reward of their shame that say, Aha, aha.

4

Let all those that seek thee rejoice and be glad in thee: and let such as love thy salvation say continually, Let ELOHIYM be magnified.

5

But I am poor and needy: make haste unto me, O ELOHIYM: thou art my help and my deliverer; O YHVH, make no tarrying.
