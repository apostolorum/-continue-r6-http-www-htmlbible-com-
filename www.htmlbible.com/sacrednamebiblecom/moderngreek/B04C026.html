[] Roubhn, o prwtotokoV tou Israhlæ oi uioi Roubhn hsan Anwc, ex ou h suggeneia twn Anwcitwnæ ek Fallou, h suggeneia twn Fallouitwnæ

6

ex Esrwn, h suggeneia twn Esrwnitwnæ ek tou Carmi, h suggeneia twn Carmitwn.

7

Autai einai ai suggeneiai twn Roubhnitwnæ kai h apariqmhsiV autwn hto tessarakonta treiV ciliadeV kai eptakosioi triakonta.

8

kai oi uioi tou Fallou hsan Eliabæ

9

kai oi uioi tou Eliab, Nemouhl kai Daqan kai Abeirwn. Outoi einai o Daqan kai o Abeirwn, oi onomastoi ekeinoi en th sunagwgh, oi stasiasanteV kata tou MwusewV kai kata tou Aarwn en th sunodia tou Kore, ote estasiasan kata tou Kuriouæ

10

kai hnoixen h gh to stoma authV kai katepien autouV meta tou Kore, en tw exoloqreumw thV sunodiaV autou, ote to pur katefage touV diakosiouV penthkonta anqrwpouVæ kai egeinan eiV shmeionæ

11

tou Kore omwV oi uioi den apeqanon.

12

Oi uioi Sumewn kata taV oikogeneiaV autwn hsan, ek Nemouhl, h suggeneia twn Nemouhlitwnæ ex Iamein, h suggeneia twn Iameinitwnæ ex Iacein, h suggeneia twn Iaceinitwnæ

13

ek Zera, h suggeneia twn Zeritwnæ ek Saoul, h suggeneia twn Saoulitwn.

14

Autai einai ai suggeneiai twn Sumewnitwnæ kata thn apariqmhsin autwn, eikosiduo ciliadeV kai diakosioi.

15

Oi uioi Gad kata taV suggeneiaV autwn hsan, ek tou Sifwn, h suggeneia twn Sifwnitwnæ ex Aggi, h suggeneia twn Aggitwnæ ek Souni, h suggeneia twn Sounitwnæ

16

ex Azeni, h suggeneia twn Azenitwnæ ex Hri, h suggeneia twn Hritwnæ

17

ex Arod, h suggeneia twn Aroditwnæ ex Arihli, h suggeneia twn Arihlitwn.

18

Autai einai ai suggeneiai twn uiwn Gadæ kata thn apariqmhsin autwn, tessarakonta ciliadeV kai pentakosioi.

19

Oi uioi Iouda hsan Hr kai Aunanæ kai apeqanon o Hr kai o Aunan en th gh Canaan.

20

Kai oi uioi Iouda kata taV suggeneiaV autwn hsan, ek Shla, h suggeneia twn Shlanitwnæ ek FareV, h suggeneia twn Faresitwnæ ek Zara, h suggeneia twn Zaritwnæ

21

kai oi uioi FareV hsan ex Esrwn, h suggeneia twn Esrwnitwnæ ex Amoul, h suggeneia twn Amoulitwn.

22

Autai einai ai suggeneiai Ioudaæ kata thn apariqmhsin autwn, ebdomhkonta ex ciliadeV kai pentakosioi.

23

Oi uioi Issacar kata taV suggeneiaV autwn hsan, ek Qwla, suggeneia twn Qwlaitwnæ ek Foua, h suggeneia twn Founitwnæ

24

ex Iasoub, h suggeneia twn Iasoubitwnæ ek Simbrwn, h suggeneia twn Simbrwnitwn.

25

Autai einai ai suggeneiai Issacaræ kata thn apariqmhsin autwn, exhkonta tessareV ciliadeV kai triakosioi.

26

Oi uioi Zaboulwn kata taV suggeneiaV autwn hsan, ek Sered, suggeneia twn Sereditwnæ ex Ailwn, h suggeneia twn Ailwnitwnæ ex Ialehl, h suggeneia twn Ialehlitwn.

27

Autai einai ai suggeneiai twn Zaboulwnitwnæ kata thn apariqmhsin autwn, exhkonta ciliadeV kai pentakosioi.

28

Oi uioi Iwshf kata taV suggeneiaV autwn hsan ManasshV kai Efraim.

29

Oi uioi Manassh hsan, ek Maceir, h suggeneia twn Maceiritwn. Kai o Maceir egennhse ton Galaadæ ek de tou Galaad h suggeneia twn Galaaditwnæ

30

outoi einai oi uioi Galaadæ ex Aciezer, h suggeneia twn Aciezeritwnæ ek Celek, h suggeneia twn Celekitwnæ

31

kai ex Asrihl, h suggeneia twn Asrihlitwnæ ek Sucem, h suggeneia twn Sucemitwnæ kai ek Semida, h suggeneia twn Semidaitwnæ

32

kai ex Efer, h suggeneia twn Eferitwnæ

33

kai Salpaad, o uioV tou Efer, den eicen uiouV, alla qugateraVæ ta de onomata twn qugaterwn tou Salpaad hsan Maala kai Noua, Agla, Melca kai Qersa.

34

Autai einai ai suggeneiai Manasshæ kai h apariqmhsiV autwn, penthkonta duo ciliadeV kai eptakosioi.

35

Outoi einai oi uioi Efraim kata taV suggeneiaV autwnæ ek Souqala, h suggeneia twn Souqalaitwnæ ek Becer, h suggeneia twn Beceritwnæ ek Tacan, h suggeneia twn Tacanitwnæ

36

kai outoi einai oi uioi Souqalaæ ex Eran, h suggeneia twn Eranitwn.

37

Autai einai ai suggeneiai twn uiwn Efraimæ kata thn apariqmhsin autwn, triakonta duo ciliadeV kai pentakosioi. Outoi einai oi uioi Iwshf kata taV suggeneiaV autwn.

38

Oi uioi Beniamin kata taV suggeneiaV autwn hsan, ek Bela, h suggeneia twn Belaitwnæ ex Asbhl, h suggeneia twn Asbhlitwnæ ex Aciram, h suggeneia twn Aciramitwnæ

39

ek Soufam, h suggeneia twn Soufamitwnæ ex Oufam, h suggeneia twn Oufamitwnæ

40

kai oi uioi Bela hsan Ared kai Naamanæ ex Ared, h suggeneia twn Areditwnæ ek Naaman, suggeneia twn Naamitwn.

41

Outoi einai oi uioi Beniamin kata taV suggeneiaV autwnæ kai h apariqmhsiV autwn hto tessarakonta pente ciliadeV kai exakosioi.

42

Outoi einai oi uioi Dan kata taV suggeneiaV autwnæ ek Souam, h suggeneia twn Souamitwnæ autai einai ai suggeneiai Dan kata taV suggeneiaV autwnæ

43

pasai ai suggeneiai twn Souamitwn, kata thn apariqmhsin autwn, hsan exhkonta tessareV ciliadeV kai tetrakosioi.

44

Oi uioi Ashr kata taV suggeneiaV autwn hsan, ex Iemna, h suggeneia twn Iemnitwnæ ex Iesoui, h suggeneia twn Iesouitwnæ ek Beria, h suggeneia twn Beriaitwnæ

45

46

kai to onoma thV qugatroV tou Ashr hto Sara.

47

48

Oi uioi Nefqali kata taV suggeneiaV autwn hsan, ex Iasihl, suggeneia twn Iasihlitwnæ ek Gouni, h suggeneia twn Gounitwnæ

49

ex Ieser, h suggeneia twn Ieseritwnæ ek Sillhm, h suggeneia twn Sillhmitwn.

50

Autai einai ai suggeneiai Nefqali kata taV suggeneiaV autwnæ kai apariqmhsiV autwn hto tessarakonta pente ciliadeV kai tetrakosioi.

51

Auth einai h apariqmhsiV twn uiwn Israhl, exakosiai ciliadeV kai cilioi eptakosioi triakonta.

[] Kai elalhse KurioV proV ton Mwushn, legwn,

53

55

56

57

[] Kai auth einai h apariqmhsiV twn Leuitwn, kata taV suggeneiaV autwnæ ek Ghrswn, h suggeneia twn Ghrswnitwnæ ek Kaaq, h suggeneia twn Kaaqitwnæ ek Merari, h suggeneia twn Meraritwn.

58

59

60

61

63
