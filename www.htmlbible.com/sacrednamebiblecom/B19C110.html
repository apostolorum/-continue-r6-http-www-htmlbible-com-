Chapter 110

1

YHVH said unto my ADOWN, Sit thou at my right hand, until I make thine enemies thy footstool.

2

YHVH shall send the rod of thy strength out of Zion: rule thou in the midst of thine enemies.

3

Thy people shall be willing in the day of thy power, in the beauties of holiness from the womb of the morning: thou hast the dew of thy youth.

4

YHVH hath sworn, and will not repent, Thou art a priest for ever after the order of Melchizedek.

5

The ADONAY at thy right hand shall strike through kings in the day of his wrath.

6

He shall judge among the heathen, he shall fill the places with the dead bodies; he shall wound the heads over many countries.

7

He shall drink of the brook in the way: therefore shall he lift up the head.
