3201

memfomai
memphomai
mem'-fom-ahee

middle voice of an apparently primary verb; to blame:--find fault.

3202

memyimoiroV
mempsimoiros
mem-psim'-oy-ros

from a presumed derivative of memfomai - memphomai 3201 and moira (fate; akin to the base of 3313); blaming fate, i.e. querulous (discontented):--complainer.
