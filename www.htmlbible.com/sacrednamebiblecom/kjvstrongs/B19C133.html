The Book of Psalms

Chapter 133

1

A Song of degrees of David.
 Behold, how good and how pleasant [it is] for brethren to dwell2 together in unity!

2

[It is] like the precious ointment upon the head, that ran down6 upon the beard, [even] Aaron's beard: that went down6 to the skirts of his garments;

3

As the dew of Hermon, [and as the dew] that descended6 upon the mountains of Zion: for there the LORD commanded14 the blessing, [even] life for evermore.
