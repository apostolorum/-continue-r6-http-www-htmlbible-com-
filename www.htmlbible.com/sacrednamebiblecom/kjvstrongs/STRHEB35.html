3501

by variation for 'yithrah' (3502); Jithra, an Israelite (or Ishmaelite):--Ithra.

3510

ka'ab

3513

kabad
kaw-bad'

or kabed {kaw-bade'}; a primitive root; to be heavy, i.e. in a bad sense (burdensome, severe, dull) or in a good sense (numerous, rich, honorable; causatively, to make weighty (in the same two senses):--abounding with, more grievously afflict, boast, be chargeable, X be dim, glorify, be (make) glorious (things), glory, (very) great, be grievous, harden, be (make) heavy, be heavier, lay heavily, (bring to, come to, do, get, be had in) honour (self), (be) honourable (man), lade, X more be laid, make self many, nobles, prevail, promote (to honour), be rich, be (go) sore, stop.

3514

kobed
ko'-bed

from 'kabad' (3513); weight, multitude, vehemence: -grievousness, heavy, great number.

3515

kabed
kaw-bade'

from 'kabad' (3513); heavy; figuratively in a good sense (numerous) or in a bad sense (severe, difficult, stupid):--(so) great, grievous, hard(-ened), (too) heavy(-ier), laden, much, slow, sore, thick.

3516

kabed
kaw-bade'

the same as 'kabed' (3515); the liver (as the heaviest of the viscera):--liver.

3517

kbeduth
keb-ay-dooth'

3518

kabah
kaw-baw'

a primitive root; to expire or (causatively) to extinguish (fire, light, anger):--go (put) out, quench.

3519

kabowd
kaw-bode'

rarely kabod {kaw-bode'}; from 'kabad' (3513); properly, weight, but only figuratively in a good sense, splendor or copiousness:--glorious(-ly), glory, honour(-able).

3520

kbuwddah
keb-ood-daw'

irreg. feminine passive participle of 'kabad' (3513); weightiness, i.e. magnificence, wealth:--carriage, all glorious, stately.

3521

Kabuwl
kaw-bool'

from the same as 'kebel' (3525) in the sense of limitation; sterile; Cabul, the name of two places in Palestine:--Cabul.

3522

Kabbown

3523

kbiyr

3524

kabbiyr
kab-beer'

3525

from an unused root meaning to twine or braid together; a fetter:--fetter.

3526

kabac
kaw-bas'

a primitive root; to trample; hence, to wash (properly, by stamping with the feet), whether literal (including the fulling process) or figurative:--fuller, wash(-ing).

3527

kabar
kaw-bar'

a primitive root; properly, to plait together, i.e. (figuratively) to augment (especially in number or quantity, to accumulate):--in abundance, multiply.

3528

kbar
keb-awr'

from 'kabar' (3527); properly, extent of time, i.e. a great while; hence, long ago, formerly, hitherto:--already, (seeing that which), now.

3529

Kbar

3530

kibrah

3531

kbarah
keb-aw-raw'

from 'kabar' (3527) in its original sense; a sieve (as netted):--sieve.

3532

kebes
keh-bes'

3533

kabash

3534

kebesh
keh'-besh

from 'kabash' (3533); a footstool (as trodden upon): -footstool.

3535

kibsah

or kabsah {kab-saw'}; feminine of 'kebes' (3532); a ewe:--(ewe) lamb.

3536

kibshan
kib-shawn'

from 'kabash' (3533); a smelting furnace (as reducing metals):--furnace.

3537

kad
kad

3538

3539

3540

Kdorla`omer

3541

3543

ko-hane'

3557

3558

3559

3562

3563

kiy
kee

a primitive particle (the full form of the prepositional prefix) indicating causal relations of all kinds, antecedent or consequent; (by implication) very widely used as a relative conjunction or adverb (as below); often largely modified by other particles annexed:--and, + (forasmuch, inasmuch, where-)as, assured(-ly), + but, certainly, doubtless, + else, even, + except, for, how, (because, in, so, than) that, + nevertheless, now, rightly, seeing, since, surely, then, therefore, + (al- )though, + till, truly, + until, when, whether, while, whom, yea, yet.

3599

3600
