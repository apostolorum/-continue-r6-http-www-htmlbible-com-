The Book of Psalms

Chapter 150

1

Praise16 ye the LORD. Praise16 God in his sanctuary: praise16 him in the firmament of his power.

2

Praise16 him for his mighty acts: praise16 him according to his excellent greatness.

3

Praise16 him with the sound of the trumpet: praise16 him with the psaltery and harp.

4

Praise16 him with the timbrel and dance: praise16 him with stringed instruments and organs.

5

Praise16 him upon the loud cymbals: praise16 him upon the high sounding cymbals.

6

Let every thing that hath breath praise17 the LORD. Praise16 ye the LORD.
