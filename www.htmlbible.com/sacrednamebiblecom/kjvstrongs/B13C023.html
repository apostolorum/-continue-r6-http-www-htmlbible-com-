12

13

14

Now [concerning] Moses the man of God, his sons were named11 of the tribe of Levi.

15

The sons of Moses [were], Gershom, and Eliezer.

16

Of the sons of Gershom, Shebuel [was] the chief.

17

And the sons of Eliezer [were], Rehabiah the chief. And Eliezer had none other sons; but the sons of Rehabiah were very many.1

18
