'Abiygayil

'Abiyah
ab-ee-yaw'

or prolonged Abiyahuw {ab-ee-yaw'-hoo}; from ''ab' (1) and 'Yahh' (3050); father (i.e. worshipper) of Jah; Abijah, the name of several Israelite men and two Israelitesses:--Abiah, Abijah.

30

'Abiyhuw'
ab-ee-hoo'

'Abiyhuwd
