7001

qtar

(Aramaic) from a root corresponding to 'qatar' (7000); a knot (as tied up), i.e. (figuratively) a riddle; also a vertebra (as if a knot):--doubt, joint.

7005

Qattath

7014

kah'-yin

the same as 'qayin' (7013) (with a play upon the affinity to 'qanah' (7069)); Kajin, the name of the first child, also of a place in Palestine, and of an Oriental tribe:--Cain, Kenite(-s).

7015

qiynah
kee-naw'

from 'quwn' (6969); a dirge (as accompanied by beating the breasts or on instruments):--lamentation.

7016

Qiynah

the same as 'qiynah' (7015); Kinah, a place in Palestine:--Kinah.

7017

Qeyniy
kay-nee'

or Qiyniy (1 Chron. 2:55) {kee-nee'}; patronymic from 'Qayin' (7014); a Kenite or member of the tribe of Kajin:--Kenite.

7018

Qeynan
kay-nawn'

from the same as 'qen' (7064); fixed; Kenan, an antediluvian:--Cainan, Kenan.

7019

qayits
kah'-yits

from 'quwts' (6972); harvest (as the crop), whether the product (grain or fruit) or the (dry) season:--summer (fruit, house).

7020

qiytsown
kee-tsone'

from 'quwts' (6972); terminal:--out- (utter-)most.

7021

qiyqayown

7022

qiyqalown

from 'qalown' (7036); intense disgrace:--shameful spewing.

7023

qiyr
keer

or qir (Isa. 22:5) {keer}; or (feminine) qiyrah {kee-raw'}; from 'quwr' (6979); a wall (as built in a trench):--+ mason, side, town, X very, wall.

7024

Qiyr
keer

the same as 'qiyr' (7023); fortress; Kir, a place in Assyrian; also one in Moab:--Kir. Compare 'Qiyr Cheres' (7025).

7025

Qiyr Cheres

or (feminine of the latter word) Qiyr Chareseth {keer khar-eh'-seth}; from 'qiyr' (7023) and 'cheres' (2789); fortress of earthenware; Kir-Cheres or Kir-Chareseth, a place in Moab:--Kir-haraseth, Kir-hareseth, Kir-haresh, Kir-heres.

Qiyshown

7029

Qiyshiy

kaw-law'

7040

Qliyta'

7049

qala`
kaw-lah'

7050

qela`

7051

7052

7054

qamah

7057

7058

7059

7071

7072

7097

7098

qatsah

7100

qetsach
