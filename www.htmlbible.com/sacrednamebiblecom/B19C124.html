Chapter 124

1

If it had not been YHVH who was on our side, now may Israel say;

2

If it had not been YHVH who was on our side, when men rose up against us:

3

Then they had swallowed us up quick, when their wrath was kindled against us:

4

Then the waters had overwhelmed us, the stream had gone over our soul:

5

Then the proud waters had gone over our soul.

6

Blessed be YHVH, who hath not given us as a prey to their teeth.

7

Our soul is escaped as a bird out of the snare of the fowlers: the snare is broken, and we are escaped.

8

Our help is in the name of YHVH, who made heaven and earth.
