Software Format

The software is in the Microsoft "HTML Help" format, like MSDN or "Help" files for software.

The software does not load anything on your computer, run a setup.exe, or update your computer's registry.

It runs entirely from the CD and does not use up your computer's hard drive space.

The software runs under the Internet Explorer browser. If you can use your browser, you can use this software.

It has a Table of Contents, an Index, a Search Engine, and a "Favorites" to keep a link to frequently studied passages.

You can copy and paste any verse into Microsoft Word by highlighting the passage with your mouse, and copying the text (control-C), and then pasting the text (control-V).
